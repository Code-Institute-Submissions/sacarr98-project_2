• Player 1 will be red and player 2 will be yellow
  • Players take turns in placing the counters into the slots, this is done by clicking on the slot you would like to place the counter
  • The aim is to get a row of four of your counters. This can be: vertical, horizontal, or diagonal
  • The first player to get four counters in a row is the winner, if no player gets four counters in a row and the slots are all filled the game is a draw.
